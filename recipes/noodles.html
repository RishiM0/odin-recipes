Simple Noodles Recipe

  • Any kind of noodles
  • Garlic
  • Scallions/Green Onions
  • Stir-fry sauce
  1. Cook the noodles according to package directions. Once the noodles are cooked, heat the oil in a large skillet then add the garlic and scallions and cook for 1-2 minutes until browned. Then add in the noodles
  2. Add the stir-fry sauce to the pan and toss everything together on medium-high heat until the noodles are evenly coated.
  3. You can optionally serve these noodles with some sesame seeds and chilli flakes on top which is delicious. You can also serve this with a sprinkle of parmesan cheese, (for a vegetarian version) which may sound odd but trust me, is delicious!
